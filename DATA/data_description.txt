The data contains 30 minute intervals data of prices of :

  1. BTCUSDT (Bitcoin - Tether)
  2. ETHUSDT (Ethereum - Tether)
  3. XRPUSDT (Ripple - Terher)
  4. LTCUSDT (Litecoin- Tether)
  5. BCHUSDT (BitcoinCash - Tether)

These are the 5 cryptos having highest market cap for the month of November 2020 according to "https://coinmarketcap.com/"

The Data was/will be downloaded using a R script directly from the Binance Exchange which is kept 
as "data_download.R" in this folder only.
